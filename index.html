Unlock the potential of Technology

At MapMitra, we believe that public transportation should be seamless, efficient, and accessible to everyone. We enhance the transit experience by bridging the gap between public transport providers and technology. As a Google Transit Partner, MapMitra offers  solutions that empower bus operators to upload their schedule and real-time data onto Google Maps, ensuring information based transit decision making for commuters.

Services

Schedule Integration

Map your bus stops, routes, and schedules with our customised solutions. We ensure that your transit data is accurately represented on Google Maps, providing passengers with reliable information to enhance their commuting experience.

Enable Real Time Tracking

Implement real time tracking  on Google maps by integrating with existing  GPS technology to enable real-time tracking of your fleet. Passengers can easily see when their bus is approaching, allowing for better planning and reducing wait times. This transparency not only enhances convenience but also ensures safety, giving commuters peace of mind knowing that their bus is on the way.

AI-Based Data Analytics

Utilize advanced AI-driven analytics to gain insights into how bus routes are being served in both rural and urban centers. Monitor key metrics such as punctuality, ridership patterns, and service efficiency to make informed decisions that enhance overall transit performance.
