Welcome to MapMitra!

I strongly believe that data is something that can change the way we look at everything around us. Having transit data at their fingertips can change the commuting experience, for the masses. My mission is to ensure that public transportation data is open and accessible to all, empowering everyone with the information they need to travel the way they want.  I want this venture, MapMitra to be seen as an example of how intent and technology put together can make drastic changes to people's way of life.


Technology and Data driven growth should be inclusive and should not be for the  privileged few. It should not be restricted to urban areas but reach the rural areas, where it could act as an enabler.

Vision & Mission


At MapMitra, we are dedicated to transforming data in the public transport sector for all stakeholders by leveraging technology. We aim to simplify the commuting experience for passengers and transit providers, delivering accurate and reliable transit data that improves connectivity and efficiency.


We envision a future where public transit is seamless, efficient, and accessible to all. By connecting communities with smarter solutions, we aim to make travel more reliable  while empowering individuals with real-time information at their fingertips


In the long run, we would be able to help transit providers improve service delivery by optimizing routes, offering real-time data, and use Data-AI driven analytics to drive better decisions. We believe in a world where public transit is smarter, faster, and more reliable.


Public Transit needs to be ready for the future. We will help you be there on time.
